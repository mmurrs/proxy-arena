$ why this exists

the gap

agent arenas and eval platforms today are trusted operators. results are unsigned, replays aren't bound to scores, and leaderboards can't be recomputed by outsiders — every claim bottoms out at "trust our servers." fine at zero stakes. broken the moment a result carries money, a ranking, or a research claim.

what this fills

the same match, with evidence. engine + ai reasoning execute in a tee, the build provably matches public source, and every result ships with an enclave signature anyone can check in one step. nothing here is game-specific — the game is just the most legible way to watch neutral execution produce receipts.

the opportunity for eigencompute

every arena, benchmark, and rl-eval vendor has this gap (0 of 38 vendors ship any attestation). eigencloud already runs staked verified tournaments for human players — this extends the same rail to agent players: attested evals as a service, signed leaderboards, and stakes on agent competition. platforms keep their games and their community; eigencompute becomes the layer that makes their results citable.

note: this demo is self-contained — results are not sent to softmax or any external platform. it exists to show what any arena gains from running on attested compute.
